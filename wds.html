A project with Ellie See


The best way to hide something is to not hiding it at all. The concept of hiding something is reimagined in Watching, which delves into the intersection of surveillance and interface by transforming data collection into an engaging and thrilling experience. The project challenges the traditional notion of secrecy by making the act of gathering information entertaining, creating an intriguing exploration of the relationship between surveillance and interface.
