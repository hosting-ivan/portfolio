Fernweh
Experience Design/Illustration

Researched with:
Sparsh Sharma
Tannia Sutanto
Yichen Liu

Traveling to a new place can be a challenging experience, especially when you are alone. Our group had the opportunity to explore a new approach to travel and examine various ways to enhance a person's journey or simply make it more convenient. We explored a range of possibilities to make an impact on travelers' adventures, considering all the different aspects that could be improved to create a smoother and more enjoyable experience.

This is the story of Fernweh.
