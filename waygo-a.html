Waygo
User Experience

Waygo is an all-in-one travel wallet built for visitors in China. Backed by China Railway, it combines secure payments, transit passes, and real-time support into a single, easy-to-use app—no local bank account required.


Tourists often struggle with fragmented payment methods, separate metro and bus cards, and unreliable support when things go wrong. Existing apps require multiple downloads, confusing flows, and opaque fee structures, leaving travelers stressed and unable to focus on their journey.


1. Seamless Onboarding
Powered by our partnership with China Railway this warm guided flow collects just what we need: passport photo, face scan and payment method in three simple steps. Friendly prompts and visible progress indicators make every interaction feel natural so you are ready to pay and ride in minutes.


2. All-in-One Travel Wallet
Tap once to switch between your payment balance and city transit passes including metro bus airport express and high-speed rail all within a single screen. Everything you need to travel with confidence in one


3. Scan to Pay Anywhere
Whether you are grabbing street food or dining in a restaurant our universal scanner and your dynamic pay code work seamlessly with WeChat Pay Alipay and other popular platforms thanks to standardized QR codes. No change needed simply point tap and go.


4. Always by Your Side
From live chat to a 24 hour hotline we speak your language English, 中文, 한국어, Español, 日本語 and we are trained to support travelers with disabilities. Reach out any time via chat phone email or our in-app guides for assistance.
