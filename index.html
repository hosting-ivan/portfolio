How it works

1 Answer a few questions ▼

Tell us about your situation and what kind of help you're looking for. Whether you need support with food, health coverage, housing, or employment, your answers help us find the right programs for you.

2 Upload documents online ▼

Submit your documents such as ID, proof of income, or residency directly from your phone or computer. This helps us verify your information quickly so your application can move forward.

3 Track status and messages ▼

Stay updated as your application progresses. You can view real-time updates, receive messages from your caseworker, and know when your benefits are ready.
