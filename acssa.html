ACSSA Self-Service Portal Bridge
User experience

Website Demo

This project connects ACSSA’s printed outreach materials with its online self-service portal. The goal is to help residents quickly understand what support is available and take the next step, whether that’s checking eligibility, applying for help, or finding local services.

Many people who need help aren’t sure where to start. They often feel overwhelmed by government terms or think applying will take too long. The infographic is designed to make that first step easier. It gives a clear overview of what ACSSA offers and points directly to the online portal through a QR code and short link.

The project moves ACSSA from general awareness to guided action. It makes applying for help feel possible and approachable. By aligning the printed and digital experience, the design builds familiarity, trust, and measurable results from first glance to final outcome.
